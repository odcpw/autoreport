## Integrierte Sicherheit (Suva)

Key ideas:
- Suva offers the “Integrierte Sicherheit” consulting program to help companies build a safety system and culture. The offer emphasizes building an effective safety system and safety culture, supported by project moderators. (Suva: Integrierte Sicherheit overview) 
- The “Modul Projekt Integrierte Sicherheit” outlines a typical project flow: Ist‑Analyse (site walk using the Führungskreislauf), training for supervisors, and coaching while implementing measures. (Suva: Modul Projekt Integrierte Sicherheit)

## EKAS 10 Elemente (ASA‑System, Richtlinie 6508)

Key ideas:
- EKAS defines the 10 elements of the ASA system (EKAS‑Richtlinie 6508) as the backbone for systematic prevention and continuous improvement. (EKAS: 10 Elemente des ASA‑Systems)
- The 10 elements are:
  1) Sicherheitsleitbild und Sicherheitsziele
  2) Sicherheitsorganisation
  3) Ausbildung, Instruktion, Information
  4) Sicherheitsregeln
  5) Gefährdungsermittlung und Risikobeurteilung
  6) Massnahmenplanung und ‑realisierung
  7) Notfallorganisation
  8) Mitwirkung
  9) Gesundheitsschutz
  10) Audit und Kontrolle

Sources:

- https://www.ekas.admin.ch/de/arbeitssicherheit-und-gesundheitsschutz/das-asa-system

## Bradley Curve (Safety Culture Maturity)

Key ideas:
- The Bradley Curve defines four maturity stages for safety culture: Reactive, Dependent, Independent, Interdependent. (dss+ Bradley Curve infographic)
- The stages emphasize a shift from reacting to incidents, to rule‑based compliance, to personal responsibility, and finally team‑based ownership. (dss+ Bradley Curve)

Source:
- https://www.consultdss.com/content-hub/dss-bradley-curve-infographic/

## ADKAR (Prosci change model)

Key ideas:
- ADKAR describes five sequential outcomes for individual change: Awareness, Desire, Knowledge, Ability, Reinforcement. (Prosci ADKAR model)
- The model emphasizes that organizational change depends on individuals progressing through these stages. (Prosci ADKAR model)

Source:
- https://www.prosci.com/adkar/adkar-model
